# PLAYMAKER Deployment URLs

## Backend (Railway)
- Copy your Railway backend URL from: Railway Dashboard → Your Service → Settings → Domains
- It should look like: `https://playmaker-production-xxxx.up.railway.app`
- Or your custom domain: `https://api.sportssense.dev`

## Frontend (Vercel)
- Will be: `https://playmaker.vercel.app` (auto-generated)
- Custom domain: `https://sportssense.dev`
